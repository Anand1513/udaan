Our Story

UDAAN Society

"We succor in restoration of lost dignity, livelihood, equality, justice and peace in favor of downtrodden communities."

UDAAN Society is registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 in December 2004 in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. Over the past two decades, we have supported critical governmental programs dealing with child labor, routine immunization, pulse polio, and HIV/AIDS awareness campaigns.

Our core areas of operational emphasis include rural health camps, groundwater recharge under Atal Bhujal Yojna, support services under the Jal Jeevan Mission, 24/7 children rescue lines, and running urban shelter homes for vulnerable individuals.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

🎯

Our Mission

To ensure all children and young people get the mentorship, education, healthcare, and safe spaces they require to regain hope, escape cycles of poverty, and thrive in life.

👁️

Our Vision

To construct a modern, inclusive, and equitable society where basic needs like clean drinking water, health services, and shelter are guaranteed to all, regardless of background.

Service, Social Responsibility, Professional Determination, Compassion, Integrity, and Community Contribution drive every project and fieldwork campaign we execute.
