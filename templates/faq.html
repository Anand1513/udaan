UDAAN stands for Unified Development Association for Amelioration of Neglected.
UDAAN Society was registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 on 13 December 2004.
The head office of UDAAN Society is located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India.
Currently UDAAN works across several districts in Uttar Pradesh including Aligarh, Bulandshahr, Firozabad, Banda, and Mahoba, and has previously executed campaigns in Rajasthan, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and the Delhi NCR region.
The organization focuses on women and children development, clean water and sanitation (Jal Jeevan & Atal Bhujal missions), livelihood support, environment protection, health awareness, and community training programs.
You can support UDAAN Society by donating through the secure Donation page, sending a direct bank transfer, or contributing to specific active campaigns.
Yes! All contributions to UDAAN Society are eligible for a 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We issue official tax certificates via email.
You can apply directly via our online onboarding form on the Volunteer Onboarding page, or browse specific open job openings on our Careers dashboard.
You can submit a message on our Contact Us page, email us directly at mail@udaansociety.org, or visit our headquarters in Aligarh.
