Professional Growth

Sharpen Social & Field Operations Skills

UDAAN Society provides exciting, structured internship opportunities for undergraduate and postgraduate students across India throughout the year. As an intern, you will directly work with field directors on campaigns dealing with water security, community health, or child protection.

Interns receive verified project experience certificates, build valuable professional networks, and get a real head start in competitive careers in development sectors.

Recommended Guidelines (Do's)

  • Be Prepared: Professional attire and mindset establish excellent first impressions in field centers.
  • Ask Questions: Clarify tasks with your field coordinator without hesitation.
  • Be Proactive: Support other project associates and take initiative on tasks.
  • Build Connections: Participate in local gatherings and network with block workers.
×

Behavioral Constraints (Don'ts)

  • Don't Be Unprofessional: Avoid casual absences or delays without notifying directors.
  • Don't Ignore Feedback: Take reviews constructively to strengthen operational capabilities.
  • Don't Hesitate: Don't keep field challenges to yourself; seek timely guidance from program managers.

Execution Procedure

  1. Step 1: Application

    Submit the online request form or email your cover letter to mail@udaansociety.org.

  2. Step 2: Slot Allocation

    We allocate slots under ongoing projects and issue an official Offer/Confirmation Letter.

  3. Step 3: Direct Mentorship

    Report to Dr. Gyanendra Mishra (Director) for briefing and field alignments.

  4. Step 4: Report Submission

    Submit your final social project report at the end of the term to qualify for certification.
