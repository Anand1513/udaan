5 DO’S:

  1. Be prepared

    First impressions count so make the effort to dress appropriately and professionally for work to leave a positive impression.

  2. Ask questions

    Never be afraid to ask questions and clarify any doubts you may have.

  3. Be proactive

    Take initiative and support your teammates whenever possible.

  4. Add value

    Offer suggestions and help your team accomplish their goals.

  5. Get involved

    Participate in social events and build strong connections.

5 DON’T’S:

  1. Don’t be unprofessional

    Maintain professionalism and avoid inappropriate behavior.

  2. Don’t be a pessimist

    Accept feedback positively and improve continuously.

  3. Don’t be late

    Be punctual and manage your time efficiently.

  4. Don’t be stressed

    Stay calm and seek help when needed.

  5. Don’t lose your temper

    Handle difficult situations calmly and professionally.

EXECUTION PROCEDURE

The following general procedure shall be adopted for the execution of the internship:

Step 1: Request Letter/Email should be sent to the mail id: mail@udaansociety.org for the internship program, email should include:

  1. Name
  2. Father’s Name
  3. Educational Qualification
  4. Permanent Address
  5. Contact Number
  6. Time period for the internship (exact date should be mentioned)
  7. Area under which he/she wants to do internship

Step 2: Organization will confirm the internship slots in that particular area under which he/she wants to do the internship via Confirmation Letter/Email.

Step 3: Students should only take the confirmation letter/Email as their offer letter for their internship program.

Step 4: Students are advised to meet Dr. Gyanendra Mishra (President Director of UDAAN Society) for further queries and internship terms.

Step 5: Interns will submit an internship report in the format provided below after completion of the internship for the issuance of the certificate.
