Recognized Excellence

UDAAN Society's Journey of Honors

UDAAN Society takes deep pride in its journey of community empowerment and resource mobilization, marked by prestigious regional and state awards. Our dedication has been honored by leading state ministers, IAS officials, municipal organizations, and medical institutions.

These achievements serve as direct validation of our fieldwork and inspire our team of coordinators and volunteers to continue pushing barriers.

Milestones

Distinguished Citations & Awards

Governor Award

Honored by the Honorable Governor of Uttar Pradesh for outstanding contributions and fieldwork in TB Eradication.

Commissioner Award

Exemplary service award presented by the then Divisional Commissioner Shri G.S. Priyadarshi (IAS) in Aligarh.

Pentagon Award

Presented at Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) by the Dr. Zakir Husain Foundation for local welfare execution.

Swami Vivekananda Samman

Awarded the prestigious Swami Vivekananda Smriti Samman in 2021 for consistent youth community outreach programs.

Nagar Nigam Award

Presented by the Municipal Commissioner, Aligarh for outstanding community contribution in the Swachh Bharat Mission.

Award for Blood Donation

Exceptional Contribution citation in public health campaigns and local emergency blood donation camps.
