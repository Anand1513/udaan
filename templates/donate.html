Why Your Donation Matters

UDAAN Society is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to restoring dignity, livelihood, equality, and justice for downtrodden communities. Founded in 2004, we work across Aligarh, Banda, Bulandshahr, Firozabad, and Mahoba in Uttar Pradesh. We implement government missions like Har Ghar Jal, Atal Bhujal Yojna, and run shelter homes, education programs, and health camps for the most vulnerable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating to UDAAN Society

Is my donation tax-deductible? +
Yes! UDAAN Society is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. All donations are eligible for tax exemption. You will receive an official receipt and 80G certificate after donating. Please share your PAN number and address when you make the transfer.
How will my donation be used? +
Your donation funds our programs including clean water access (Jal Jeevan Mission), shelter homes, children's education, health camps, women's empowerment, and community livelihood programs across Uttar Pradesh.
Can I donate in-kind (goods, materials)? +
Yes, we accept in-kind donations such as food, educational materials, clothing, and medical supplies. Please contact us at {{ contact_email }} to arrange in-kind donations.
Can my organization make a corporate donation? +
Yes! We welcome CSR partnerships and workplace giving programs. Please visit our Workplace Giving page or contact us directly for corporate donation arrangements and partnership opportunities.
Will I receive a receipt for my donation? +
Absolutely. After your bank transfer, email us at {{ contact_email }} with your transaction reference. We will send an official donation receipt and your 80G certificate within 3–5 working days.
What is UDAAN's registration number? +
UDAAN Society is registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 on 13 December 2004. Our head office is located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. You can verify our registration at the local Registrar of Societies office in Aligarh.
