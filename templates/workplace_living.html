Employee Workplace Giving with UDAAN

Workplace giving initiatives provide a powerful opportunity to support underprivileged communities where your business operates, while strengthening bonds with your team. Corporate employees are encouraged to help through donation matching, structured payroll deductions, and coordinated field volunteering.

What is Workplace Giving?

Workplace giving enables employees to make small, recurring financial or time contributions to registered NGO causes, directly supported or matched by the employer.

Multi-stakeholder Benefits

A structured workplace philanthropy program provides significant benefits to employers, employees, and NGOs.

🏢

Benefits for Employers

  • ✔ Attracts values-driven top talent.
  • ✔ Enhances team engagement and cooperation.
  • ✔ Lowers turnover metrics (studies show up to 52% reduction in turnover with purpose-led programs).
👥

Benefits for Employees

  • ✔ Direct, hassle-free monthly philanthropy.
  • ✔ Lowers taxable income under 80G tax exemptions.
  • ✔ Aligns professional life with humanitarian efforts.
🤝

Benefits for UDAAN

  • ✔ Reliable, steady source of monthly support.
  • ✔ Ability to execute long-term water management.
  • ✔ Close collaborations with regional companies.
